V. Suthakar — Earth & Space Science, York University

Tracking what we leave behind in Earth orbit.

I'm Vithurshan Suthakar, a PhD candidate in Earth and Space Science at York University. My research builds optical detection and image-processing pipelines for Space Situational Awareness — from ground telescopes to stratospheric balloon payloads and on-orbit star trackers.
